# Coffee Shop Sales Analysis Dashboard
# Project Overview
This Power BI dashboard privides a comprehensive analysis of sales data for a retail coffee shop.The goal is to track key performance indicators (KPIs), understand revenue trends, and identify the impact of external 
factors like weather and product categories on overall business performance.
## Business Questions Addressed
In this project, l aimed to solve thwe following business questions:
* ** What are the high-level performance metrics, including **Total Revenue**, **Total Orders**, **Average Sales Per Order**, and **Discount Utilization**?
* ** Sales Trends:** How does the daily revenue flcutuate over time, and what is the overall growth trend?
* ** Product Performance:** Which spesific products are the top revenue contributors (**Top 5 Analysis**)?
* ** Environmental Impact:** Is there a correlation between **weather conditions** (Sunny,Rainy,Snowy)?
* ** Category Distribution:** How is the total revenue distributed across different product categories (Coffee, Tea, Bakery, etc.)?

## Key Insights
* ** Revenue Leader:** "Coffee" is the dominant category, contributing the largest share of total revenue (over 40%), followed by "Tea" and "Sandwitches">
* ** Weather Sensivitiy:** Sales performance shows a positive correlation with **Sunny** weather, while **Rainy/Snowy**  days a slight dip in transaction volume.
* ** Sales Stability:** Despite daily fluctuations, the overall sales trend remains stable with a healthy **Average Sales(AOV) of 6.90**.

* **  Tool:** Power BI Deskop

* ** Dax (Data Analysis Expressions ):** Created custom measures for **Average Sales** and **Discount Rate %**.
